Gift registry
-------------

We're very fortunate to have everything we need, so please do not bring
gifts. In case you would like to bring a card or a personal note, we'll have
a space for them at the picnic. If you would like to give a gift, we ask that
you support one of our interests by donating to one of the following
charities:

* `Equity in Education (UNCF) <https://secure2.convio.net/uncf/site/SPageNavigator/2016Donate.html>`_
* `Health (Children's Hospital Los Angeles) <http://connect.chla.org/site/TR?px=2463466&fr_id=1090&pg=personal>`_
* `Health (Jodi's Race for Ovarian Cancer Awareness) <https://www.jodisrace.org/ovarian-cancer-awareness>`_
* `Health (The ALS Association) <https://secure2.convio.net/alsa/site/Donation2?39808.donation=form1&idb=593220411&df_id=39808&FR_ID=14329&mfc_pref=T&PROXY_ID=8214378&PROXY_TYPE=20&set.Value=undefined&s_urlSetters=true&set.DonationLevel=84717>`_
* `Outdoor Recreation (Access Fund) <https://www.accessfund.org/join-or-give/support-access-fund>`_
* `Scientific Curiosity (Denver Museum of Nature and Science) <https://secure1.dmns.org/donate-to-the-annual-fund-today?ref=dmnsorg_header-bar_button>`_
