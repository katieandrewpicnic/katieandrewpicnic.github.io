Katie and Andrew’s anniversary picnic¶

We got married! Get ready to celebrate!

Location and schedule¶

The picnic will be on Saturday, August 13, 2022, which is the 2nd anniversary of our wedding. We’ve reserved the “Spring Valley” site at Ed Levin park in Milpitas. The coordinates for the parking lot are (37.447128146075066, -121.84686819815745) [linked here].

You can arrive as early as 11am. Lunch will be around noon, and dessert will be around 1pm. We’ll have to start cleaning up by 5pm, but don’t feel obligated to stay that late.

What to wear¶

This will be a casual picnic, not a wedding ceremony. We would like everyone to wear what makes them comfortable. Keep in mind that we’ll be playing lawn games, so if you’d like to participate in the lawn games please wear something suitable. We expect that it will be pretty hot in Milpitas in August, so consider bringing a hat and sunscreen and dressing lightly.

What to bring¶

Please bring what you need to be comfortable outside, such as a hat and sunscreen. We will have outdoor space for things like lawn games, so please bring any lawn games you like. It’d be helpful if you can bring your own camping chair or blanket to sit on.

There is a $6 vehicle entry fee to get to the picnic site. We recommend bringing cash and carpooling if possible.

What not to bring¶

Many of our guests have allergies and dietary restrictions. Please do not bring any of the following:

  1. tree nuts (including almond and coconut products)

  2. peanuts

  3. latex (including balloons)

  4. furry pets (sorry!)

Additionally, the park prohibits the following

  1. smoking

  2. drones

  3. glitter

If you’re wondering about gifts, please see the gift registry section.

Gift registry¶

We’re very fortunate to have everything we need, so please do not bring gifts. In case you would like to bring a card or a personal note, we’ll have a space for them at the picnic. If you would like to give a gift, we ask that you support one of our interests by donating to one of the following charities:

  • Equity in Education (UNCF)

  • Health (Children’s Hospital Los Angeles)

  • Health (Jodi’s Race for Ovarian Cancer Awareness)

  • Health (The ALS Association)

  • Outdoor Recreation (Access Fund)

  • Scientific Curiosity (Denver Museum of Nature and Science)

Health and Safety¶

To ensure that everyone, including those with health concerns can attend safely, everyone must be vaccinated against COVID-19 before attending the picnic. Please do not attend if you are not feeling well.
