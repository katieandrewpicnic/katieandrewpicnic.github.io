What to bring¶

Please bring what you need to be comfortable outside, such as sunscreen. We also hope to have outdoor space for things like lawn games and will let you know if you can bring those once we have a time and place set.

What not to bring¶

Many of our guests have allergies and dietary restrictions. Please do not bring any of the following:

  1. tree nuts (including almond and coconut products)

  2. peanut products

  3. latex (including balloons)

  4. furry pets (sorry!)

If you’re wondering about gifts, please see the gift registry page.
